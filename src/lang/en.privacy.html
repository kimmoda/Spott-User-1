Appiness - SPOTT - Privacy Regulations

  1. Introduction
    1. These Privacy Regulations describe the tasks and responsibilities of Appiness NV, with its registered office at Hertshage 10, 9300 Aalst, Belgium and registered with the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises under number 0543.611.457 (Register of Legal Persons Dendermonde) (“Appiness”) with regard to your Personal Data collected via the mobile application SPOTT (“SPOTT”) by Appiness and Processed by the Controller of the Processing.
    2. Personal Data protection is very important to Appiness. Appiness respects your privacy and ensures that your Personal Data is Processed in accordance with the applicable legislation, in particular the provisions of the Belgian Law of 8 December 1992 on the protection of privacy with regard to the processing of personal data and the Implementation Decree of 13 February 2001 (the “Privacy Act”).
    3. These Privacy Regulations describe which information is collected and Processed, the purposes of the Processing, with whom Personal Data may be shared and the choices you can make regarding the use of your Personal Data by Appiness. It also sets out the measures which are taken to ensure the security of your Personal Data and how you can contact Appiness to exercise your rights in relation to access, correction and objection.
    4. By downloading, installing or using SPOTT, you confirm you have received, read and understood these Privacy Regulations and that you have accepted their content. More specifically, you agree that Appiness:
      1. may collect your Personal Data and Process it in the manner and for the purposes as set out in these Privacy Regulations;
      2. may disclose your Personal Data to third parties;
      3. may make use of analytics solutions of third parties in SPOTT and on the Website, particularly but not exclusively Google Analytics;
      4. may make use of proprietary software, such as log files, to record the activity of SPOTT;
      5. may encode your Personal Data and combine it with the Personal Data of other SPOTT Users in order to compile statistics relating to the general use of SPOTT;
      6. may make use of cookies and other technologies on its Website.
    5. If you do not agree with any provision of these Privacy Regulations, you should refrain from downloading, installing, using or opening SPOTT. You can oppose the Processing of your Personal Data at any time in accordance with these Privacy Regulations or withdraw your consent by interrupting the installation of SPOTT or by removing SPOTT from your mobile device.

  3. The Collected Personal Data
    1. When downloading, installing or using SPOTT, you will provide certain Personal Data to Appiness, such as your name, address, date of birth, gender and prompted preferences for certain brands, products, services or promotional activities. When you choose to register with SPOTT via the login data of certain social networks, such as Facebook, you explicitly give Appiness permission to collect these authentication data.
    2. You agree to only provide Appiness with accurate, current and complete Personal Data. You further agree to update your Personal Data to keep these accurate, current and complete. Appiness reserves the right to terminate your right of use of SPOTT if the Personal Data provided by you are incorrect, inaccurate or incomplete.
    3. In addition, certain Personal Data may be automatically collected, such as:
      1. technical data relating to your mobile device, in particular a footprint of the mobile device, the number of the mobile device, mobile applications on the mobile device, the name of the mobile device, the e-mail address installed on the mobile device, the identifier, the OS version, the name of the telecom operator, the telephone number, the SIM card number, IMEI, IMSI, etc.;
      2. information about your interactions with SPOTT, with other SPOTT Users and with Applications of Third Parties;
      3. User Content you post in SPOTT.
  4. The Purposes of the Processing of the Collected Personal Data

    5. Appiness collects and Processes the aforementioned Personal Data, as Controller of the Processing, for the following purposes:

      1. to make SPOTT and the Services available to you, including the downloading, installing, registration, using and access to SPOTT;
      2. to operationally manage SPOTT, including offering products and services via SPOTT;
      3. to deal with questions and complaints regarding SPOTT and to communicate with you for purposes related to SPOTT;
      4. to block access to SPOTT, or suspend or interrupt the Services of SPOTT for security purposes;
      5. to make further updates of SPOTT available;
      6. to analyse and report on the use of SPOTT and Applications of Third Parties;
      7. to allow you to participate in competitions via SPOTT;
      8. to validate and send your orders for certain services or products via Applications of Third Parties;
      9. to make the interaction with television content possible;
      10. to personalise and improve your experience of SPOTT and the Services, as well as with Applications of Third Parties;
      11. direct marketing and market research.
  5. Sharing the Collected Personal Data and Information
    1. The User Content that you post in SPOTT, is shared by you with Appiness, other SPOTT Users and/or Applications of Third Parties and will always be publicly available. Any wish list that you keep in SPOTT, containing a list of products or services that interest you, will be made public only if you expressly choose this in the settings of SPOTT.
    2. Unless otherwise stated, in these Privacy Regulations, Appiness will not disclose or transfer the collected Personal Data to third parties unless you give your prior express consent.
    3. Appiness may share the collected Personal Data with third party service providers in order to perform tasks, to Process Personal Data and to help provide the Services, in accordance with these Privacy Regulations. When a third party service provider Processes Personal Data in its own name (where the third party service provider acts as Controller of the Processing), the Processing is subject to the privacy regulations of such third party service provider and the applicable law.
    4. Appiness may transmit the collected Personal Data to third party service providers who act as Processors in the name of and on behalf of Appiness (where Appiness acts as Controller of the Processing). Appiness requires that such Processors only Process your Personal Data if they are instructed to do so by Appiness and requires that they protect the safety and confidentiality of Personal Data, in accordance with these Privacy Regulations and the Privacy Act.
    5. Appiness reserves the right to disclose your Personal Data (i) in response to requests for information from public authorities or regulators, or where otherwise required by law; (ii) to enable a merger, acquisition or sale of part or all of its assets; and (iii) to issue anonymous or aggregated information about the use of SPOTT.
    6. For the above purposes, Appiness may transfer your Personal Data to countries other than the country in which it was originally collected. Your Personal Data, however, will only be transferred within the European Economic Area or to countries approved by the European Commission as countries that can demonstrate they have a sufficient level of Personal Data protection. When Appiness transfers your Personal Data to other countries, it will protect this information as described in these Privacy Regulations.
  6. Safety of Personal Data
    1. The safety of Personal Data is important to Appiness. As Controller of the Processing, Appiness implements appropriate technical and organisational safety measures to protect the safety and confidentiality of your Personal Data. Appiness also ensures that any other third party service providers that it engages implements appropriate and organisational safety measures to protect the safety and confidentiality of your Personal Data.
    2. Appiness has taken internal and organisational measures to prevent Personal Data being made accessible to and Processed by unauthorised persons and maintains physical, electronic and procedural safeguards to secure Personal Data.
    3. Appiness applies, if it considers this necessary, security measures that are consistent with industry standards to protect Personal Data, such as identification and password requirements.
    4. Appiness strives to protect privacy and confidentiality of Personal Data by means of suitable safety measures, but Appiness cannot always guarantee the complete safety of the Processing of Personal Data, for instance where the Processing of Personal Data takes place via the internet or other electronic communication platforms.
    5. Your password protects your user account You are required to use a unique and strong password, restrict access to your mobile device and log off after using SPOTT.

  8. Analysis and Statistics
    1. Appiness may decide to integrate analytics tools into SPOTT and the Website which - subject to your prior consent where required by law - third parties providing analyses (such as Google Analytics) will be allowed to collect information on the use of SPOTT, your mobile device and the Website with a view to providing aggregated reports to Appiness to gain insight into the use of SPOTT and the Website and how they can be improved.
    2. Appiness may make use of analytics solutions of third parties on the Website, particularly but not exclusively Google Analytics; Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics uses anonymous trackers (a random set of data used for the same purposes as cookies on platforms) to track and analyse the use of the Website. The information generated by the Website (including language, regional information [country, region, city], device and network information [make, model, service provider, system]) is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States of America. Google will use this information to measure your interaction with the Website, to analyse the use of the Website and to compile reports on the Website activities. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required by law or if third parties Process the information on behalf of Google. Appiness will use anonymous and aggregate reports from Google for the sole purpose of evaluating the use of the Website and to improve the Website and SPOTT.
    3. Appiness may make use of log files in order to record the activity of SPOTT; This information is collected and processed solely for statistical purposes and in order to continue to improve SPOTT.
    4. Appiness may also encode your data and combine it with the data from other SPOTT Users to compile statistics relating to the general use of SPOTT, which enables it to develop new products and services. The combining of these data always takes place on an encoded and completely anonymous basis. Appiness may also share this aggregated data with its business partners, advertisers and other third parties.
